Our mission with CareWear

Our mission with CareWear is to make the daily lives of people that have memory disorders much easier. Our product will make sure that the individuals' lives will be much easier, safer and practical, because of all the functionalities that comes with the product.

Martin Tran

My work explores the relationship between the challenges of ageing and midlife mindfulness at the intersection of achievement. With both personal experience and influences as diverse as a father and a grandfather, new synergies are synthesised from both personal and external experiences. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the essential unreality of the human condition. What starts out as triumph soon becomes finessed into a spaghetti of daily lifestyle challenges, the stubbornness of ageing, leaving only a sense of failing and the possibility of a new synthesis. As subtle achievements become unravelled through diligent and repetitive practice, the end user is left with a tribute to the outposts of our existence.
